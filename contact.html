Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Smart Farming System

How do I get started with Smart Farming System?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account on our platform, set up your farm profile, and you'll have access to all our features including weather forecasts, crop management tools, soil analysis, and marketplace. We offer a free 30-day trial for new users.

Is the soil analysis tool accurate?

Our soil analysis tool provides recommendations based on standard agricultural practices and scientific data. For precise results, we recommend laboratory testing, but our tool gives excellent guidance for most farming needs. Many farmers have reported 40-50% yield improvements using our recommendations.

How much does the platform cost?

We offer multiple pricing plans: Free (basic features), Premium (‚Çπ499/month) for individual farmers, and Enterprise (custom pricing) for large farms and cooperatives. All paid plans include priority support and advanced features.

Can I sell my crops through your marketplace?

Yes! Our marketplace connects farmers directly with buyers. You can list your crops, set prices, and manage sales. We charge a minimal 2% transaction fee for successful sales. All payments are secure and we provide delivery coordination services.

Do you offer training for new farmers?

Absolutely! We provide free online training modules, webinars with agricultural experts, and in-person workshops in major farming regions. Check our "Learning Center" section for upcoming training sessions and recorded tutorials.
