Crop Diseases & Treatments

Identify and treat common crop diseases

Blast Disease

Rice

Symptoms: Spindle-shaped spots on leaves, neck rot

Cause: Fungus (Magnaporthe grisea)

Treatment: Tricyclazole, Azoxystrobin

Rust

Wheat

Symptoms: Orange-brown pustules on leaves

Cause: Fungus (Puccinia spp.)

Treatment: Propiconazole, Tebuconazole

Late Blight

Potato/Tomato

Symptoms: Dark lesions on leaves, fruit rot

Cause: Fungus (Phytophthora infestans)

Treatment: Metalaxyl, Chlorothalonil

Use Resistant Varieties

Plant disease-resistant crop varieties suited to your region.

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops to break disease cycles and reduce pathogen buildup.

Sanitation

Remove infected plant debris and clean tools regularly.

Proper Irrigation

Avoid overhead watering to reduce leaf wetness duration.

Organic Treatments

  • Neem Oil: Effective against many fungal diseases
  • Baking Soda Spray: For powdery mildew control
  • Garlic-Chili Spray: Natural insect repellent
  • Copper Fungicide: Approved organic treatment

Chemical Treatments

  • Systemic Fungicides: Absorbed by plants for lasting protection
  • Contact Fungicides: Protect plant surfaces
  • Bactericides: For bacterial diseases
  • Always follow label instructions and safety precautions
